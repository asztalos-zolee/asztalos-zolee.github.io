Fleet

Privately owned fleet of VOLVO tractors and SCHMITZ and KRONE curtainsider trailers. 
 
Our fleet of tractors, 95% of which are EUR 6 environmentally compliant models, consists of vehicles an average of 1.5 years old.
 
All trailers have lifting roofs for easy crane loading.  Load heights vary from 2.8 to 3.0 metres.  Trailers are Code XL certified and are equipped with the necessary means for securing cargo (straps, mats, and bars).
 
In addition to 30 privately owned tractor-trailers, we also have at our disposal the vehicles made available by our long-term charter subcontractors.
 
Each vehicle is additionally equipped with Dynafleet, Volvo’s own factory satellite communication system.
